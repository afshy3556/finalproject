Our Institute

Government Institute of Information Technology Lahore established on 29/11/2006 is proud of being the first ever information technology institute of TEVTA by the Punjab Government, which is being managed/supervised by the District Manager/Zonal Manager North TEVTA Lahore. The institute is a remarkable contribution/service of TEVTA, Government of Punjab, for the people of Lahore District. The institute is affiliated with Punjab Board of Technical Education (PBTE) Lahore, Trade Testing Board (11th Lahore), and City & Guilds UK for the award of certification.
